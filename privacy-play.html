Penguin Math Race: Kids Game

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Penguin Math Race: Kids Game is developed and published by CopperHand Studio, an independent developer located in Türkiye, operating under the Third Axis Games creative label.

1. Overview

Our priority is providing a safe and fun learning environment for children. This app is free to play and supported by family-friendly advertisements. We comply with strict data protection laws, including COPPA.

2. Data Collection (COPPA Compliance)

We do not knowingly collect any personal information (such as name, email, or phone number) from users. For parents: This app uses Google AdMob. We have configured all ad requests for Child-Directed Treatment (COPPA).

The Google AdMob SDK may process certain non-personal technical data:

  • Device Identifiers: Used strictly for frequency capping and fraud prevention.
  • Network Information: To check internet connectivity.
  • Approximate Location: Derived from IP address for security and safe ad delivery.

3. Advertising and Families Policy

We strictly adhere to the Google Play Families Policy:

  • We only use Google Play Certified Ad SDKs (AdMob).
  • We serve only Non-Personalized Ads (not based on user behavior).
  • Ad content is filtered for general audiences (G) and children.

4. Safety & External Links

To ensure a safe environment, the app contains: No In-App Purchases, No Social Media Links, and No User Chat features.

5. Intellectual Property & AI Usage

All game materials were created by CopperHand Studio. Some assets were produced with the assistance of licensed AI tools, reviewed and finalized by the developer. CopperHand Studio asserts full ownership for in-game use.
